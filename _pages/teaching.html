📋 Teaching Philosophy & Resources

My teaching approach focuses on:

  • Hands-on Learning: Practical exercises and real-world applications
  • Interactive Engagement: Discussion-based learning and collaborative projects
  • Progressive Difficulty: Building concepts step-by-step with clear foundations
  • Modern Tools: Using current industry-standard tools and methodologies
  • Open Communication: Encouraging questions and providing timely feedback
